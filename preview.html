Business Insights & HR Recommendations

Key Findings

  • Overall attrition rate is 16.12%.
  • Sales and Human Resources show higher attrition rates than Research & Development.
  • Employees working overtime show substantially higher attrition risk.
  • Sales Representative and Laboratory Technician roles require closer retention focus.
  • Younger employees and lower-income employees show higher attrition tendency.

Recommended HR Actions

  • Reduce overtime pressure in high-risk teams.
  • Conduct stay interviews for high-risk employees.
  • Create role-specific retention plans for Sales and Laboratory roles.
  • Improve onboarding and mentoring for employees in their first 2 years.
  • Review compensation and growth paths for low-income employee groups.
